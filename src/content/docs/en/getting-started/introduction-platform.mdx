**Intabia Platform** is an open source all-in-one project management platform.

**Intabia Platform** is a powerful suite of features including workflow management, personal scheduling, collaborative editing and video conferencing.

**Intabia Platform** streamlines project management processes in a way that aligns seamlessly with the complexity and dynamic nature of real-world workflows.

This documentation contains information about all of the main platform features.

## Getting started

If this is your first time using **Intabia Platform**, get started by following our guide for setting up your workspace! Then, check out some of our most popular features, including:

- [Action items](/en/task-tracking/creating-action-items)
- [GitHub integration](/en/integrations/github)
- [Virtual office](/en/communication/virtual-office)

## Where we're headed

As we hone in on our goals for the future of **Intabia Platform**, we're focusing on creating a better process management system. We're re-working the entire framework of **Intabia Platform** to lay the foundation for a platform that will be a convenient tool for managing work processes.

Our development is largely driven by user feedback, and we're committed to delivering an exceptional user experience while making room for fresh ideas and a different way of looking at tools.

Welcome to **Intabia Platform** — we're excited to grow with you!
